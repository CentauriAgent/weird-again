# Weird Again 🌀

> **"The internet remembered how to be fun."**

A Nostr onboarding site for Gen-Z and normies. [weirdagain.com](https://weirdagain.com)

---

## What Is This?

**Weird Again** is a marketing and onboarding site designed to bring the next wave of users to Nostr — specifically targeting Gen-Z and everyday people who are fed up with algorithmic social media but haven't found an alternative that actually feels good.

The pitch: Nostr is social media where you own your account, your content, and your identity. No ads. No algorithm. No corporate overlords. And it's actually fun.

---

## The Problem We're Solving

Half of Americans cut back on social media in 2025. Gen-Z is actively looking for something different. But every "alternative" platform either:
- Died after the hype faded
- Got bought and enshittified anyway
- Went full Web3/crypto and became a grift

Nostr is different. It's a protocol, not a platform. Nobody owns it. Nobody can buy it. And the apps built on it are genuinely creative and fun.

## Key Messaging Principles

- **Fun first, freedom second.** Lead with the vibe, back it up with the values.
- **The email analogy.** Best way to explain Nostr to normies: "Imagine if social media worked like email."
- **No jargon.** No "decentralized protocol," no "public-key cryptography," no "relay servers."
- **Not Web3.** Nostr has no tokens, no gas fees, no speculation. Distance from crypto culture is intentional.
- **Anti-enshittification, not anti-corporate.** The tone is empathetic and fun, not angry.

See [`CONTENT-BRIEF.md`](./CONTENT-BRIEF.md) for the full messaging guide, tone rules, and what NOT to say.
